Who I am

My name is Nicolò Cavalieri, and I am passionate about building innovative projects from robotics and AI models to open-source tools that make life simpler. I focus on robotics, modeling, hardware, and optimal control, always eager to take on new challenges. I've explored multi-agent systems and reinforcement learning, and I'm a big fan of open-source, mechatronics, HMI, cockpit and helmet-mounted display simulators, autonomous vehicles such as drones, helicopters, AGVs, AMRs, and humanoids.

Making technology both fun and accessible is one of my core motivations.
I'm constantly working to improve my professional skills by studying topics of interest through online resources and courses from top-tier providers. Professionally speaking, I aspire to become an Embedded Software Engineer with strong AI and Computer Vision expertise, aiming to design autonomous flying vehicles, robots, or humanoids as a Software Engineer.

Recently, I've been learning how to build websites using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, and I plan to design my own PCBs in the near future.
My ultimate goal is to become a well-rounded Software Engineer with a focus on software rather than hardware.
